About Us

The story behind Kitabkhana

We believe every book changes a life

Kitabkhana — meaning "library" in Nepali and Urdu — was founded with one simple belief: great books should be within everyone's reach. We started as a small shelf in Kathmandu and grew into Nepal's favourite online bookstore.

Whether you're a seasoned reader or just picking up your first novel, we're here to help you find the story that speaks to you.

10,000+ Books Available
50,000+ Happy Readers
7+ Genres Covered
5 Years Serving Nepal

Our Mission

To make quality books accessible to every reader in Nepal — at fair prices, with fast delivery, and with a handpicked selection that spans every genre and interest.

Our Vision

To build a reading culture across South Asia — where books are not a luxury but a daily companion, and where every home has a bookshelf worth admiring.

Our Values

Honesty, curiosity, and love for the written word. We believe knowledge is the most powerful thing you can carry — and books are how you carry it.
